About Sam's Jazz Class

Sam is very passionate about dancing and teaching dance. The Jazz class Sam has designed for ages 10 - 16 yrs old includes classical dance elements, contemporary and a whole lot of jazz! Dance technique is taught through the class warm-up that will strengthen young dancers and improve their flexibility. If you want to get into splits this is the class that will have your legs around your ears in no time!
Students will learn a lot of flashy dance tricks during diagonal work across the dance floor and then put all their hard work into a routine.
Sam is very dedicated to her students and treats every student as an individual with different needs.

About Sam the Dancer

Sam was a professional dancer for 17 years before she retired from performing to solely focus on teaching.
As a commercial dancer she has been a dancer for Take That, Simply Red, Yazz, Pauline Henry, Rozalla. She has appeared on the Brit Awards and Top of The Pops and many pop videos. She has also performed in fashion, hair and trade shows for Adidas, Alternative Hair Show, Citroen Cars. She has performed in West End shows Saturday Night Fever, Fame, Fosse to name a few and also the movie Delovely starring Kevin Kline and John Barrowman.

About Sam the Teacher

Sam has taught professional advance jazz classes at Pineapple and Danceworks to other professional dancers. She also taught at colleges and was Head of Jazz at Stella Mann Dance College for several years. Her teaching abilities are recongised at top colleges such as Arts Education School, Urdang and Laban as well as locally at Studio 74 dance school before she was encouraged to start her own school. Sam is still currently in demand guest teaching at dance colleges.
