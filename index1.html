About Sam Modeste

Sam As A Professional Dancer

She was a professional dancer for 17 years before she retired from performing to solely focus on teaching. Sam has worked in Paris at the world famous Paradis Latin dance cabaret show. She has toured with Take That and appeared in many pop videos. She has performed in West End shows Saturday Night Fever, Fame, Fosse to name a few.

Sam's Class

Sam is a versatile dancer and teacher. Her classes incorporate the current Jazz styles.

Commercial Jazz

A style used for dancing with pop groups. For those students inspired by pop videos.

Lyrical & Contemporary Jazz

For those students inspired by Dance Moms or The Next Step tv shows.
